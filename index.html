Forget coding and the wheel - It's a scientific fact that mankind's best achievement is the pizza. This humble dish is celebrated the world over so allow me, if you will, to take up a little corner of the internet to sing its praises once more!

Pizza Facts!

  • The first online pizza order was made in 1974 when scientists at Michigan State University ordered a pizza to test their AI Lab's new 'speaking computer'!
  • Italians eat around 1.6 billion pizzas every year!
  • The average Brit will eat a whopping 731 pizzas throughout their lifetime! (Incidentally, the average Norwegian gets through 5kg of pizza every year...)
  • The technical term for the pizza crust is the cornicione!

Pizza Recipes!

Since its invention in the 18th century, there have been many different styles and varieties of pizza from pineapple to chocolate! At the last count, official lists show there are 687 different varieties of pizza and more are being invented all the time! While it's widely accepted that there are 7 main types of Italian pizza there are plenty of other varieties from South America to Cyprus and I'd like to share some recipes so you too can get a slice of the action in your own kitchen!
